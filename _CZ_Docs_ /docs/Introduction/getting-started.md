CloudZero is the cloud cost intelligence platform that puts cost into context for your business. You can set up in minutes to get cost information and anomalies flowing to your email and Slack channels.

# Set Up CloudZero

1. Go to [app.cloudzero.com](http://app.cloudzero.com) and create an account.
2. Connect a [Cost Source](/docs/connections) to CloudZero.
3. Set up [Slack integration](/docs/enabling-slack-integration).
4. Watch your team maximize profitable innovation with cloud cost intelligence!

# Add users

Anyone in your company can go to [https://app.cloudzero.com](https://app.cloudzero.com) and sign-up for an account. Anyone with the same email domain will be added to your organization.

# More help

Reach out to CloudZero at [support@cloudzero.com](mailto:support@cloudzero.com) and the team will be happy to assist in any way possible.
